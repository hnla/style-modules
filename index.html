Welcome to the Styles Modules pages

Here you can find details on using and installing style modules or authoring snippets for the BP community. 'Snippets'? Snippets is simply a reference to a shorter block of code in our case these snippets are small modular styles that target specific aspects or components of your BP site, as an example the 'Members List Module' to transform the lists into grid like boxes.
